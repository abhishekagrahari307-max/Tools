How It Works

Our Image Enhancer tool uses advanced algorithms to optimize your images while preserving quality. Here's how to use it:

  1. Upload your image by dragging and dropping or clicking to browse files
  2. Adjust the compression level to find the perfect balance between quality and file size
  3. Select your desired output format (JPEG, PNG, or WebP)
  4. Click "Enhance Image" to process your image (takes just a few seconds)
  5. Download the optimized version of your image

All processing happens directly in your browser - we never upload your images to our servers, ensuring complete privacy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this tool free to use?

Yes, our Image Enhancer tool is completely free to use with no hidden costs. You can process as many images as you need without any limitations.

Are my images uploaded to your servers?

No, all image processing happens directly in your browser. We never upload your images to our servers, ensuring complete privacy and security for your photos.

What's the maximum image size I can process?

You can process images up to 10MB in size. For best results, we recommend using images between 1-5MB. Larger images may take longer to process.

Which format should I choose for my images?

WebP generally offers the best balance of quality and file size. JPEG is good for photos, while PNG is better for images with transparency or needing lossless compression.

Can I use this tool on my mobile device?

Absolutely! Our tool is fully responsive and works perfectly on smartphones and tablets as well as desktop computers.
